✅ Pay by Check – Checklist

🔔 Trigger

  • The customer requests to make a payment using a check, money order, MoneyGram, or cashier's check.

📬 Mailing Address to Provide

  • Instruct the customer to mail the check to:
    • Affirm Inc – Customer
      PO Box 201209
      Dallas, TX 75320-1209

📋 Instructions to Provide to Customer

  • Check must be made payable to: Affirm or Affirm Inc.
  • Include the following on or with the check:
    • The phone number associated with their Affirm account
    • The Loan ID for the loan they are paying
  • A separate check is required for each loan
  • Inform the customer:
    • It may take up to 10 business days from the date of receipt to process
    • They will receive a text and email confirmation once the check has been applied

📂 Salesforce Case

  • No case is required unless:
    • The customer reports a problem with the check
    • Or requests confirmation/follow-up for mailed payments

📉 Collection Records

  • Create Collection Record(s) only if a related issue is discussed
For any confusion or latest updates, please refer to the official procedure PDF.
