✅ Direct Complaint – Checklist

🔔 Trigger

  • A customer (or their representative) expresses dissatisfaction with Affirm's products, services, policies, or actions — either verbally or in writing. This also includes complaints related to harassment or regulatory issues.

☎️ TCN Actions

  • No TCN changes required unless combined with Cease & Desist or UserComm update

📂 Salesforce Case Creation

  • Create case type: Direct Complaint
  • Fill out Dynamic Fields:
    • Product ID: [Loan ID if applicable]
    • Additional Comments: Detailed explanation of the customer's complaint and context
    • Status: In Progress
  • Reassign Case to: Next Team
  • Do not create child cases (not required)

📉 Collection Records

  • Create Collection Record(s) (if applicable to the complaint case)

📣 Special Cases – Harassment-Related Complaints

  • If the customer mentions harassment via a specific channel (calls, emails, SMS):
    • Follow Update UserComm Preferences procedure
    • Then create a Direct Complaint case
  • If the customer says "Affirm is harassing me" without specifying the channel:
    • Follow Cease & Desist procedure
    • Then create a Direct Complaint case

📣 Customer Notification

  • Let the customer know:
    • Their concern is being documented and escalated for review
    • A follow-up may occur within a few business days if necessary
For any confusion or latest updates, please refer to the official procedure PDF.
