✅ Customer Refuses to Pay – Checklist

🔔 Trigger

  • Customer clearly states they refuse to pay their loan
  • e.g., "I'm not paying," "You won't get a dime from me," etc........

☎️ TCN Actions

  • Stop calls on TCN: Never
  • TCN Disposition: Refuse to Pay

📂 Salesforce Case Creation

  • Create a case type: Cease and Desist
  • Fill out Dynamic Fields:
    • User Collections Updated: Disabled: Other
    • Additional Comments: e.g., "Customer refused to pay – [Your Name + Initial] / [Date]"
    • Status: Closed

👥 Child Cases

  • Create a child case for each associated account
  • Follow same steps for each

🛠️ Agent Portal

  • Apply the Cease and Desist Tool on the customer's profile
For any confusion or latest updates, please refer to the official procedure PDF.
