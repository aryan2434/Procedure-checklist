✅ Unable to Pay – Checklist

🔔 Trigger

  • The customer states they are currently unable to make a payment or are experiencing temporary financial hardship.

☎️ TCN Actions

  • Stop calls for 1 month on TCN
  • TCN Disposition: Temporary Hardship

📂 Salesforce Case Creation

  • Create case type: Unable to Pay
  • Fill out Dynamic Fields:
    • Future Payment Scheduled: No – Unable to pay
    • Product ID: [Loan ID]
    • Form of Payment Relief: Other
    • Payment Activity Requested: Unable to Pay
    • Requestor: Account Holder
    • Loan Modification Discussed: No
    • Reason for Delinquency: [Based on customer's explanation]
    • Partial Payment Completed: Yes/No (as applicable)
    • Status: Closed
    • Additional Comments: Summarize discussion and list all loan IDs involved
  • Do not create child cases for associate accounts

📉 Collection Records

  • Create Collection Record(s)
