✅ Red Flag Interaction – Checklist

🔔 Trigger

  • You observe suspicious, inconsistent, or potentially fraudulent behavior during a customer interaction. This may include third-party involvement, mismatched identity info, or attempts to gain unauthorized access.

📂 Salesforce Case Creation

  • Create case type: .Red Flag Interaction
  • Fill out Dynamic Fields:
    • Contact is the Account Holder: [Yes/No – based on verification outcome]
    • Response Expected: No
    • Additional Comments: Provide a detailed summary of the call and red flag indicators. Include your name and the date.
    • Status: In Progress
  • Reassign Case to: Next Team
  • Do not create child cases for associate accounts

📉 Collection Records

  • Create Collection Record(s) if applicable

📋 Examples of Red Flag Behavior

  • Caller switches from “my” to “their” when referring to the account
  • Caller whispers to someone else during verification
  • Claims a family member opened the account without permission
  • Third party attempts to gain access to the account
  • Asks for sensitive access information (e.g., SMS or email verification codes)

⚠️ Reminder:

  • Do not assume anything based on voice or gender
  • Only escalate when clear suspicious patterns are observed
