✅ Reverse Payment – Checklist

🔔 Trigger

  • Customer requests reversal of a payment made via debit card or bank account.

💳 If Payment was via Debit Card:

  • ☎️ Customer Confirmation
    • Confirm payment was made within 3 calendar days
    • Confirm customer agrees to 3–10 business days for funds to reflect back
  • 💬 Rocket Chat Request
    • Post request in Rocket Chat:
    • Hello, can someone pls help me with a reverse payment?
      ARI: [insert ARI]
      Loan ID: [insert loan ID]
      Amount: [insert amount]
      Cx agreed with the terms.
  • 📂 Salesforce Case (only if needed):
    • Create case only if:
      • No Rocket Chat response
      • Payment made by bank account

🏦 If Payment was via Bank Account:

  • 📞 Customer Confirmation
    • Confirm 3–5 business days have passed since payment was initiated
    • Confirm customer agrees to 3–10 business days refund timeline
    • Educate customer on refund expectations
  • 📂 Salesforce Case Creation
    • Case Type: Reverse Payment
    • Fill out Dynamic Fields:
      • Product ID: [Loan ID]
      • Status: In Progress
      • Payment Activity Requested: Reverse payment – Pending or Processed
      • Requestor: Account Holder
      • Payment Already Processed: Yes/No
      • Is Contact within 3 days of payment: Yes
      • Payment Reversed: Yes/No
      • Additional Comments: Include ARI, loan IDs, amounts, and customer agreement
  • 🧾 Case Reassignment
    • Reassign to: First Team
  • 📉 Collection Records
    • Create Collection Record(s)
For any confusion or latest updates, please refer to the official procedure PDF.
